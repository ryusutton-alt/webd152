The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
A round, sturdy tent structure with a skylight cozy, weather-resistant, and set on a deck.
How are the yurts furnished?
Comfortable bed and linens, seating, electric lighting, and basic amenities for a relaxing stay.
What should I bring?
Layers for changing coastal weather, walking shoes, and your sense of adventure.
