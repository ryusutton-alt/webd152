Activities

Hiking

Explore coastal trails with guides who point out native plants, tidepools, and scenic overlooks.

Kayaking

Paddle calm coves and watch for sea lions and shorebirds. Beginner-friendly sessions available daily.

Bird Watching

Join a morning walk with our naturalist to spot pelicans, cormorants, and seasonal migrations.

What to Bring

  • Sun protection and layers
  • Water bottle and snacks
  • Comfortable walking shoes
