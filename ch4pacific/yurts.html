The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our Pacific Trails Resort yurts are a modern adaptation of an ancient nomadic shelter. Each yurt is a round tent-like structure with wood floors and a permanent roof.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is fully furnished with comfortable beds, a gas fireplace, and a private deck with breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean.
What should I bring?
Bring casual clothing and a sense of adventure! All linens and basic toiletries are provided.
